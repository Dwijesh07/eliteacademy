Our Teaching Methodology

Evidence-based approaches that deliver measurable results

The Elite Academy Approach

1

Diagnostic Assessment

Every student begins with a comprehensive diagnostic assessment to identify knowledge gaps, learning preferences, and strengths. This includes academic testing, learning style questionnaires, and goal-setting interviews.

2

Personalized Learning Plan

Based on diagnostic results, we create a customized learning plan with specific, measurable goals. This plan is aligned with the Victorian Curriculum and tailored to the student's school requirements.

3

Scaffolded Instruction

We use a scaffolded approach, breaking complex concepts into manageable steps. Instruction follows the "I Do, We Do, You Do" model, gradually releasing responsibility to the student.

4

Multisensory Engagement

Lessons incorporate visual, auditory, and kinesthetic elements to engage different learning styles. Technology integration enhances learning through educational apps and interactive resources.

5

Feedback & Adaptation

Continuous formative assessment provides real-time feedback. Learning plans are regularly reviewed and adjusted based on progress, ensuring optimal learning trajectories.

6

Metacognitive Development

We explicitly teach learning strategies, self-monitoring techniques, and study skills to develop independent, self-regulated learners.

Research-Based Practices

Our methodology is grounded in educational research, incorporating:

  • Cognitive Load Theory
  • Spaced Repetition
  • Retrieval Practice
  • Growth Mindset Principles
  • Differentiated Instruction
  • Formative Assessment Strategies
